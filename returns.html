Returns, Shipping & Policies

Shipping

Many items qualify for free shipping. We use USPS, UPS, or FedEx with tracking. Local pickup available in Springfield area by appointment.

Returns

30-day return window for most items (buyer pays return shipping unless item is significantly not as described). Contact us within 30 days of delivery.

Platform-Specific

Each marketplace (eBay, Amazon, etc.) has its own buyer protection and return policies. We manage returns on your behalf as the consignor.

Full policies are provided at time of purchase and available upon request.
