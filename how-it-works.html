How Selling Works

Your guide to consigning and selling online the easy way

1

Create Your Listing

Sign up for a vendor plan and add your item with photos, description, and CIA condition rating. You can consign almost anything — clothing, electronics, collectibles, auto parts, and more.

2

We List Everywhere

Once submitted, we automatically list your item on our online store, eBay, Amazon, Whatnot, and Facebook Marketplace. One listing, maximum exposure.

3

Item Sells

When a buyer purchases, the order appears in your dashboard. You'll get a notification with buyer details and shipping instructions.

4

Ship or Pickup

Print your shipping label from the dashboard, or schedule a pickup from your home. We support FedEx, UPS, and USPS. Hand-write the address if you prefer.

5

Get Paid

After the buyer receives their item, your earnings are deposited. Track everything from your seller dashboard.
