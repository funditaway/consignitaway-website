About Consign It Away

Founded in Springfield, Missouri, Consign It Away helps people turn their unwanted items into cash without the hassle of photographing, listing, shipping, and customer service on multiple platforms.

Our team (and AI) evaluates each item, rates its condition using our trusted CIA scale, and lists it across eBay, Amazon, Whatnot, Facebook Marketplace, our own store, and more — all from one simple form.
