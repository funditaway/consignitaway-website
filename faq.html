Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI choose platforms for my item?

Our system analyzes category, condition, price, and demand data to recommend the best marketplaces (eBay, Amazon, our store, etc.). You can override the suggestions when consigning.

What happens after I consign an item?

We photograph (if needed), list it on the recommended platforms using official APIs, handle inquiries, and ship when sold. You get paid when the buyer receives it.

How much do I earn?

You set your price. We take a small commission (varies by plan). Starter plan is free up to 50 listings. See vendor-plans.html for details.

Can I connect my own eBay/Amazon account?

Yes! Go to the Connections page after logging in. We use official OAuth flows so you stay in control.

What if my item doesn't sell?

Listings stay active based on your plan. You can edit price, photos, or withdraw the item anytime from your dashboard.
